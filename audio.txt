Ever fell out of place in a conversation, like everyone sounds smarter than you?
Here's the truth. Sounding smart isn't about using big words. It's about how you communicate.
Here are three simple tricks, one, ask better questions. Instead of just nodding, ask,
what led you to that conclusion. It makes you sound insightful and engaged.
Pause before you speak. Smart people don't rush. A sure pause before responding makes you
seem thoughtful and confident. Keep it simple, complexity is an intelligence. The smartest
people explain things clearly and simply. Master these and you'll never feel lost in a conversation
again. Try them out.
